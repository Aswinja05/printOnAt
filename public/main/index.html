    About Us

    Have you ever faced the hassle of crowded printer shops, waiting endlessly, or the struggle of sending documents via email or WhatsApp just to get them printed? We understand the frustration—and that's why we created PrintOnAt, your seamless printing companion. With PrintOnAt, you can upload your files directly on our platform, specify your preferences such as paper type, size, and color, and select a nearby printer shop that is most convenient for you. You can even pay online, eliminating the need to fumble with cash. That’s it! Simply pick up your prints at your selected shop and get back to your work or class without delay.
